About Me

Since I was young, I had a lot of hobbies, such as watching movies, playing games, sports, etc.

I heard that "A human has two lives, the second one begins when you realize you only have one life." Then I started to think about how to fulfill my life and have no regrets.

After that, I found that many challenges were too dangerous, such as parkour because there is a risk of death. Then I found out that GAMES are based on the virtual world; if you can make it feel more realistic, then it will be no different than real life but it won't risk your life into.
